Where I've Lived

Most of my childhood was spent in Asia, as I moved from London to Singapore at 6 weeks old. After 4 years in Singapore I moved to Chicago, Illinois in the US
for two years befroe returning to Singapore. Due to the amount of time that I have been in Asia, most of my favourite places
are in Asia, including tropical islands and the Indonesian jungle. Along with Asia, the list also includes the likes of South Africa, Australia and my home country, England.

To the List

The tropical islands
of Asia

These islands, for example Bali, have some of the most clear water in the world, with magnificent, clean beaches and coral reefs with every species of sea creature and colour of coral that you can possibly imagine.

South Africa

With so much diversity of culture and landscape, South Africa proves to be one of the most spectacular places in the world, whether you are looking out across Cape Town from the peak of Table Mountain, or are admiring the African Penguins on the shingle beaches.

Thiepval War Memorial, Belgium

The monument was built shortly after World War I to commemorate the soldiers that fought and died on both sides during the battle of Thiepval, one of the worst of the war in terms of lives lost. Inscribed in the bricks are over 72,000 names of soldiers and officers, with many of the graves standing around the memorial itself.
